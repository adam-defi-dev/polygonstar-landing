Yield Farming

</h1>

<p>

To encourage a variety of investors to farm on our platform we have implemented a unique rewards system. We believe that investors should always be in total control of their investment strategy and the rewards offered should always be proportional to each investor’s exposed risk.

</p>

<h1>

DEX & Liquidity Pool

</h1>

<p>

The Polygon Star will also have a decentralized exchange (DEX) where $PoS holders can contribute liquidity to traders and earn transaction fees from the pool. Pool fees will be set at 0.20% and will be available on the Polygon Star dapp interface.

</p>

<h1>

Auto Compounding

</h1>

<p>

One of the key features of Polygon Star will be its ability to provide an auto-compounding function for various farms on the Ethereum chain with the Pulse chain soon to follow. When you stake LP tokens into our vaults, the smart contract deposits them into the underlying farms (e.g. Sushiswap, 1inch Swap, etc) for rewards and it will then automatically harvest and compound into more LP tokens for our users.

</p>

PoS Token Economics

PoS token is the main utility & governance token on Polygon Star. To ensure that the price of $PoS is stable and owners are incentivized to hold we have implemented the following stabilizing mechanisms:

  • Trading tax – Applied to each trading (3%) of the $PoS
  • Deposit Fees - A fee which is assessed when entering non-$PoS based farms or pools
  • Dymond Perks – An incentive program for holding $PoS (wordplay on both Diamond and $PoS)
